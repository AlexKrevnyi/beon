    How to Find Serial Number on your Apple Watch

    1. Remove the band from your watch, then look inside the band slot.

    How to find the Serial Number or IMEI

    1. On your Apple Watch, press the Digital Crown to go to the home screen.
    2. Tap on the "Settings" app (it looks like a gear icon).
    3. Scroll down and tap on "General".
    4. Tap on "About".
    5. Scroll down until you see the "Serial Number" option.
    6. The number next to "Serial Number" is your Apple Watch's serial number.
    7. You can also find the serial number of your Apple Watch on the original packaging, on the receipt, or in the Apple Watch app on your iPhone.
