    How to Find Serial Number or IMEI of your iPhone, iPad, or iPod Touch

    1. On your iPhone, go to the Home Screen and tap on the "Settings" app.
    2. Scroll down and tap on "General" in the settings menu.
    3. In the General menu, scroll down and tap on "About".
    4. On the About page, look for the "Serial Number" option. It should be the fifth or sixth option from the top. You might need to scroll down to find the IMEI/MEID
    5. Tap on the "Serial Number" option to see your iPhone's serial number.
    6. If you need to copy the serial number, you can tap and hold on the number to copy it to your clipboard.

    How to Find Your IMEI or Serial Number if you don't Have Your Device

    1. Open a web browser on your computer and go to iCloud.com.
    2. Sign in to your iCloud account using your Apple ID and password.
    3. Once you're signed in, click on the "Find iPhone" icon.
    4. Click on the "All Devices" dropdown menu at the top of the page and select the iPhone for which you need to find the serial number or IMEI.
    5. Once you've selected the device, a window will appear with information about the iPhone, including its model, serial number, and IMEI.
    6. If the device is currently offline or not connected to the internet, the serial number and IMEI may not be displayed.
